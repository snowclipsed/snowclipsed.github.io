Hi, I am snow/snowclipsed. My irl name is Hardik.

Welcome to my little corner on the internet. I am a machine learning engineer based in the Boston, MA area. I specialize in deep learning architecture research and am interested in inference optimization.

currently

I’m pursuing a MSCS @ Khoury College of CS, NEU, where I am researching synthetic photometry data generation for astronomical imaging. I am also working at a top lab in my city where I am using vision transformers to do remote photoplethysmography.

previously

In summer ‘24, I spent some time detecting fish in rivers with MIT. In fall ‘23, I moved to the United States after finishing up my undergrad and internships, then published a physics paper in winter.

goals

I intend to be really good at scaling ML models (distributed systems), making good VLMs work on really low-end devices and ML arch. These goals keep me up at night. Currently I am diving into CPU and GPU arch and programming, and low level optimizations.

on the side

When I’m not working, I enjoy making games and sharing my thoughts on my blog. In my free time, I like to post on 𝕏, consume and make art, listen to music, play video games, and write.
