# Movement

I like things that move,

a city's vibrant hum.

'scrapers reaching their hands,

to pluck the clouds' colored plum.



In nature's grand theater, a tale unfolds, 

Where varies, secrets it holds. A flower's delicate unfurling, at dawn's first light, A gradual dance of petals, a slow, exquisite flight.

Mountains rising over eons, a patient sculptor's hand, Layers of history formed, in rock and sand. Glaciers creeping through epochs, carving valleys wide, A testament to patience, as landscapes slowly bide.

Stars that shimmer in the night, distant and bright, Their journeys vast and timeless, a cosmic flight. Galaxies drifting apart, across ages they roam, In the boundless expanse, finding their own home.

The tide's rhythmic embrace on the tranquil shore, Shaping cliffs and beaches, a process to adore. A dance with the moon, a ballet over years, Creating art in sands, a story that perseveres.
