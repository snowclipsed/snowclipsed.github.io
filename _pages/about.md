Hi, I am snow/snowclipsed. My irl name is Hardik.

Welcome to my little corner on the internet. I am a machine learning engineer based in the Boston, MA area. I specialize in deep learning architecture research and am interested in inference optimization.

#### currently

I'm pursuing a MSCS @ [Khoury College of CS, NEU](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/), where I am researching synthetic photometry data generation for astronomical imaging. I am also working at a top lab in my city where I am using vision transformers to do remote photoplethysmography. 

#### previously
In summer '24, I spent some time [detecting fish](https://seagrant.mit.edu/2020/04/02/mit-sea-grant-announces-five-newly-funded-projects/) in rivers with MIT. In fall '23, I moved to the United States after finishing up my undergrad and internships, then published a physics paper in winter.

#### goals

I intend to be really good at scaling ML models (distributed systems), making good VLMs work on really low-end devices and ML arch. These goals keep me up at night.
Currently I am diving into CPU and GPU arch and programming, and low level optimizations.
