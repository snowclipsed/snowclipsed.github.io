Hey there! I'm Hardik, better known as <i>snow/snowclipsed</i> online.

Welcome to my little corner on the internet.

I'm a graduate CS student at the [Khoury College of Computer Sciences](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/) and dev based in Boston, MA. This summer, I've been diving into GPU programming and spending some time [detecting fish](https://seagrant.mit.edu/2020/04/02/mit-sea-grant-announces-five-newly-funded-projects/) in rivers with MIT.

I love technology and enjoy tinkering with it to make life easier. I like the thrill of learning new things - this leads to fun and (often) useful projects. I like things image processing, machine vision, NLP and language models, game dev. 

Lately I've been learning CUDA programming, machine learning optimization, and MLOps infrastructure. I intend to be really good at scaling models to millions of users.

When I'm not working, I enjoy making games and sharing my thoughts on my blog. In my free time, I like to post on [X](x.com/snowclipsed), consume and make art, play video games, and [write](/blog/).
