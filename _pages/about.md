Hey there! I'm Hardik, better known as <i>snowclipsed</i> online. Welcome to my little corner on the internet. 

Currently, I'm a graduate CS student at the [Khoury College of Computer Sciences](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/) and dev based in Boston, MA. This summer, I've been diving into GPU programming and spending some time [detecting fish](https://seagrant.mit.edu/2020/04/02/mit-sea-grant-announces-five-newly-funded-projects/) in rivers.

Previously, I worked at Deloitte as a Machine Learning Engineer, where I handled the planning and deployment of natural language systems. This experience taught me a lot about AI at scale. I also have research experience and recently published a [paper](https://www.frontiersin.org/articles/10.3389/fphy.2024.1322162/full).

I love technology and enjoy tinkering with it to make life easier. I like the thrill of learning new things - this leads to fun and (often) useful projects.

When I'm not working, I enjoy making games and sharing my thoughts on my blog. In my free time, I like to consume and make art, play video games, and write.
